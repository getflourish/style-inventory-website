From Exploration to Specification

Design is the mastery of meaningful order, organisation and structure. But to get there, design requires free, sometimes chaotic exploration.

Design software like Sketch can handle both aspects, but moving from exploration to structured layouts with text styles and unified colors can be time-consuming.

Style Inventory is a set of plugins that will help you tidy up your Sketch files. At any time, you can get a visual overview of your used styles. By seeing how often and where you use those styles, it will also help you to indentify unneccessary styles and thus make it easy to merge similar styles.
